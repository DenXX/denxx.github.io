My CV

I am a research scientist at Facebook, working on natural language understanding in Messenger. I've received my Ph.D. degree from Emory University, where I worked at the Intelligent Information Access Lab with Prof. Eugene Agichtein. Currently, my research interests are in the areas of natural language understanding, semantic parsing, dialog systems and question answering.
